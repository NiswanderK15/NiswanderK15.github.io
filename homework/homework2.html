Here's a poem about my brother: Don’t Let The Door Swing Closed

Sit with me. Swing and tell me what’s on your beautiful, brilliant, bursting mind. No Bugs Life 300, Goodbye Shrek. You are my favorite person. Funny and full of life that bursts at the seams of your Genie shirt. We swing. The wind picks up over the pond. Watch the sunset cast shadows over your fair face. I hold your hand. It’s cold to the touch from the evening air. Your hazel eyes do not meet mine. I love you, Wesley, with every ounce of heart I have. Sitting on this swing with you is a holy sort of memory. You live a life so different than mine but you always hold the door to let me back in.
